Complete guide for Indian students planning to study MBBS abroad in 2026. Covers eligibility, fees, admission process, top countries, and career opportunities.# MBBS Abroad Guide 2026 for Indian Students

Studying MBBS abroad has become a popular option for Indian students due to affordable fees and global exposure. Countries like Russia, Kyrgyzstan, and Kazakhstan offer quality education with simple admission processes.

## Why Choose MBBS Abroad?
- Affordable tuition fees
- No donation required
- Globally recognized degrees
- English medium programs

## Important Considerations
Students should also understand licensing exams like FMGE/NExT required to practice in India after completing their degree.
